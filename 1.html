There are 264 and 214 electoral votes each for Joe Biden and incumbent Donald Trump from the states already called the election for the candidates.

The swing states of Nevada and Georgia have been reporting 87% and 99% each out of the entire votes including mail-in ballots, with which Joe Biden is leading the race by about 13,000 and 3,000 votes each at the moment.

The swing state of North Carolina has called the election for Donald Trump, in which he wins by about 80,000 votes.
